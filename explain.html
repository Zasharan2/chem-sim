Chemistry Simulation

Explanation

Welcome to the bonding chemistry simulation! In this simulation, you can create elements and bond them together in various ways.

Within the simulation environment, you may create atoms by pressing spacebar and typing the atomic number of that which you wish to create. Press enter, and voila! Your new atom will appear. These atoms may be clicked and dragged around. To form bonds, simply click two electrons in succession.

When two atoms have multiple bonds, you may click the two bonds to form a bond group, used to represent double and triple bonds. Bonds and bond groups may be undone by pressing backspace.

DISCLAIMER: This simulation is not an accurate representation of molecular bonding. This is only a tool for better conceptualization of bonds, and should not be used as a resource to make empirical determinations.
